So much of my adult life, every year could be neatly summarized by "did my job, cooked dinner, raised babies". I don't think there was much value in summarizing that life. But this year, in all of the freedom of not working a 9-5 job I feel detached from time. When reading Rek's [year in review](https://kokorobot.ca/site/2025dec31.html) I found myself wondering "Gosh, what did happen this year?".
